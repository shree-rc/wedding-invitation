A Divine Love, Guided by Allah

Asking Allah for guidance and praying for a righteous spouse is encouraged, as the
Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) said:
"O Allah, I ask You for the good of the one I seek to marry, and the good You have destined for us."

The journey towards marriage in Islam is not just about the wedding day, but about creating a lifelong bond built on faith, trust, love, and mutual respect. It’s about bringing two people together under the guidance of Allah, with the intention to live a life of worship, build a home of peace, and raise righteous children. It’s a journey that requires patience, effort, and reliance on Allah for success and happiness.

The Proposal
